About iZi

iZi is a gamified open “school”, founded in Vietnam since 2021 with 100K+ organic users in 66+ countries. iZi is preparing the official launch in July 2022 in both web and mobile app. With iZi:

  • An expert can host a live broadcast to a large community and record it, with fascinating gamification tools.
  • A working professional can join and interact dynamically in a live broadcast or replay it asynchronously with friends.
